Projects

  • Straw Poll

    Strawpoll is a site where users have the ability to create instant, real-time polls. The site lets anyone create a poll with a question and multiple options. The creator of the poll then can copy the url and share with their friends and watch the votes roll in. The results page shows the list of options ranked by number of votes with a bar showing the percents each has received.
